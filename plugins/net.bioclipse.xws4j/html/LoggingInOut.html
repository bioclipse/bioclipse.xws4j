Logging in and out of the XMPP-service

To log in to the all you have to do now is to login to your Bioclipse user account. If all goes well then the padlock in the status line (at the bottom of the Bioclipse window) will turn green and the light-bulb in the toolbar will turn yellow.
When you log out from our user account, you will automatically be logged out from the Jabber service. See more about login and out and how to handle your user account in the Bioclipse user manual.
While your logged into your user account you can disconnect and reconnect to to the Jabber service Connect in the menu that you get when clicking on the small triangle on the right side of the light-bulb in the tool bar. Or via using the commands "XMPP.connect()" and "XMPP.disconnect()" in the JavaScript console. Observe that you can not login to the Jabber-service if your not logged in to your Bioclipse account.
See also the section about login in and out from your user account.
